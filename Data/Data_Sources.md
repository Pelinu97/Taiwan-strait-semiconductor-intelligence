## Methodological Note

Figures cited in /analysis (e.g., TSMC revenue, China node-level progress, EU Chips Act targets)
are time-sensitive and were last verified as of mid-2026. Where a report cites a specific figure, 
it is sourced to a dated report rather than treated as a fixed fact — readers revisiting this project
later should check the linked sources for updates rather 
than assume the cited figures still hold.
